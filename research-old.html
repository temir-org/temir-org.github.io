Research on modeling, paraphrasing, and summarizing language, as well as argumentation, conversation, and causal knowledge.

Completed Projects

BMBF Project: Center for Scalable Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (ScaDS.AI): Subprojects on bias analytics, and web mining and crowdsourcing for scalable training data acquisition. [2019 - 2022]
Research on bias in machine learning-based systems and on the large-scale acquisition of training data through distant supervision.
Partners: 14 partners at Leipzig University and Dresden University

Development of an AI laboratory that allows for the cloud-based experimentation with AI systems and their evaluation.

BMBF Project: Phoenix – Scientific Authorship in Flux: Development of new Performance Criteria in Science [2019 - 2022]

Partners: C. Schendzielorz (DZHW), B. Stein (Bauhaus-Universität Weimar)
